Content Moderation Policy

Last Updated: December 20, 2025

1. Our Commitment

You And I, Not AI is committed to providing a safe, authentic, human-only dating environment. We actively moderate content and user behavior to maintain platform integrity.

2. Prohibited Content

Zero Tolerance (Immediate Ban + Law Enforcement Report):

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sex trafficking or exploitation
  • Threats of violence
  • Terrorism or extremist content

Immediate Account Termination:

  • AI-generated messages or bot usage
  • Underage users (under 18)
  • Fake profiles or catfishing
  • Non-consensual intimate images
  • Harassment or bullying
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Spam or commercial solicitation
  • Scams or fraud attempts

3. AI/Bot Detection

Our core mission is human-only connections. We detect and remove:

  • AI-generated profile photos
  • ChatGPT/LLM-generated messages
  • Automated messaging scripts
  • Bot accounts
Warning: Using AI to write your messages or generate your photos is a permanent ban offense. We use advanced detection systems.

4. Reporting

Users can report violations via:

  • In-app report button on any profile or message
  • Email: safety@youandinotai.com

All reports are reviewed by human moderators within 24 hours.

5. Response Times

  • CSAM/Trafficking: Immediate removal + law enforcement
  • Violence threats: Within 1 hour
  • Harassment: Within 24 hours
  • AI/Bot reports: Within 24 hours
  • General violations: Within 48 hours

6. Enforcement Actions

  • Warning: First minor offense
  • Temporary Suspension: Repeated minor offenses (7-30 days)
  • Permanent Ban: Serious violations or 3+ warnings
  • Law Enforcement Referral: Illegal content

Bans are not appealable for: CSAM, trafficking, AI usage, or underage users.

7. Appeals

For eligible suspensions, you may appeal within 14 days by emailing:

appeals@youandinotai.com

Include your username and explanation. Appeals are reviewed within 5 business days.

8. Transparency

We publish quarterly transparency reports including:

  • Number of accounts removed
  • AI/bot detection statistics
  • Law enforcement requests
  • Appeal outcomes

9. Contact Safety Team

Email: safety@youandinotai.com

Emergency: If someone is in immediate danger, contact local law enforcement (911 in the US).
